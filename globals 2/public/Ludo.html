📖 How to Play
Master Globals Ludo
1

Roll the Dice

Tap "Roll Dice" on your turn. Need a 6 to get tokens out of base.

2

Move Tokens

Tap any eligible token to move. Tokens go clockwise. Get all home to win!

3

Capture Opponents

Land on opponent to send them home! ⭐ Safe squares protect tokens.

4

Roll 6 = Extra Turn

Rolling 6 gives another roll! Enter tokens or chase opponents.

5

10-Second Timer

After rolling, a bar shrinks on your panel. Zero = turn passed.

6

Challenge Mode

Pay an entry fee to face a real opponent. Win the prize!

📜 Terms & Conditions
⚠️ Globals Ludo includes real-money entry fees. Read before playing.

1. Eligibility

Must be 18+ to play real-money modes. By playing you confirm this and that it's legal in your jurisdiction.

2. Fair Play

All games use certified randomness. Manipulation or exploitation leads to immediate termination.

3. Entry Fees

Entry fees are deducted before matching. Prizes are credited on completion. All transactions are final.

4. Responsible Play

Only enter amounts you're comfortable with. Globals is not liable for losses from irresponsible play.

5. Deposits

Deposits are handled through the main Globals platform. Ludo shows your real-time balance only.

6. Disconnection

Intentional disconnects during a challenge forfeit the match. Accidental disconnect allows 10s reconnect.
